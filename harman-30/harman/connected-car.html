TELEMATICS

HARMAN mobile connectivity solutions set the industry standard for keeping drivers and passengers in touch with the world around them.

Telematics describes a wide range of automotive connectivity solutions that leverage a telematics control unit (TCU) incorporating some form of cellular communication to create an automatic system.

CONNECTED SAFETY

HARMAN is developing a complete range of connected safety offerings that help drivers keep their eyes on the road and hands on the wheel.

We begin by keeping drivers intimately aware of their surrounds with rear-view and wide-angle camera applications. Virtual reality modeling is then blended with the camera images, to create a full surround view of the vehicle from any angle for safer driving and parking efficiency.
