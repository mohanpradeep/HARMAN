MUSIC

Inspiring young people through contemporary music education.

HARMAN believes that the power of music can inspire each of us to realize our possibilities. Music is a globally relevant and universal experience, and is a proven way to nurture creativity as well as teach critical academic and life skills. With our long history in music innovation, we at HARMAN apply our expertise, time and technologies in our corporate social responsibility activities to make a positive impact on the lives of young people

Through HARMAN Inspired, we bring contemporary music education to students of all ages. We do this by partnering with leading nonprofits around the world. In the U.S., HARMAN is the lead Technology Partner for Little Kids Rock, the largest organization providing innovative music education, to reach more than 400,000 underserved public school students in grades K-12. Together, we integrate technology into their "Modern Band" curriculum and overall music-making learning model. As part of our CSR activities in India, China, Germany and Hungary, HARMAN collaborates with music education organizations to enhance and modernize their youth programs. In addition, HARMAN supports key music industry staples such as NAMM and AES, as well as the brilliant and impactful work of our brand ambassadors like A.R. Rahman and Linkin Park.

TECHNOLOGY

Inspiring young people through relevant, hands-on STEM learning experiences.

HARMAN believes that all young people should have the opportunity to learn a robust set of science, technology, engineering and math (STEM) skills to succeed. Numerous studies have shown (and our employees across the globe have seen) how important early access to STEM education is for providing career pathways for our future generations. As a connected technologies company, HARMAN is passionate about advancing the STEM education arena through our corporate social responsibility activities, and bringing our technology expertise, resources and inventive drive to young people across the globe.

Through HARMAN Inspired, we support nonprofit programs and universities that deliver cutting-edge, relevant STEM skills-building experiences. In the U.S., HARMAN partners with the Square One Education Network to fuel ten “V2X Research and Development Lab Schools” in Michigan. In these labs, middle and high school students create connected car technologies through an engaging, hands-on robot vehicle design experience while exploring potential careers. HARMAN also invests in innovative programs and competitions with leaders such as the XPrize, the Wadhwani Foundation and the Society of Women Engineers. In collaboration with local universities, HARMAN holds technology challenges such as the HARMAN Tech Challenge in Hungary where young people showcase their capabilities in product design.

SERVICE

Engaging in impact-driven volunteerism across the globe to improve lives.

HARMAN believes in the power of service and volunteerism. Our employees have a long-held passion for supporting the communities in which we live and work in, through individual and team activities. HARMAN's employees around the globe address local needs by applying our expertise across CSR activities including conducting hands-on service, hosting food and clothing drives and organizing fundraisers to advance critical social issues with nonprofits of all sizes.

Through HARMAN Inspired, employees participate in diverse "Signature Days" that focus on specific issues such as music education, STEM and climate change.

We also come together to tackle pressing challenges brought to us by our nonprofit partners. These corporate social responsibility service activities leverage our employees' interests, our reach and our inventive drive throughout the year. Hour of Code, International Women's Day, Make Music Day and Earth Day are just some of our focused events where we seek to create change.

Service is one important way HARMAN connects its employees across the world, builds leadership and collaboration skills, and gives back to our local communities.
